About Us

Ibrahim Catering Service is the most trusted catering service in Thanjavur, specializing in Mutton Biryani, known locally as Bhai Veetu Biryani. We can serve meals for up to 1500 guests and customize the menu to your needs.

Our mission is to provide delicious, hygienic, and memorable meals for all occasions, making your events unforgettable.
